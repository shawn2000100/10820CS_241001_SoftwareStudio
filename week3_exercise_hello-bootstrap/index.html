Why?

The first challenge you face everyday is to get up from your warm, lovely bed. If you fail sometimes, it's not your fault—your alarm is just too boring to remind you how wonderful the day is. And this is why we build a new app for you:

FriendlyAlarm, yet another social alarm that wakes you up on time, everyday.

Features

STEP 1:
Set an alarm with a friend. You don't need to sleep or wake up at the same time.

STEP 2:
Say something to your friend to help her get up; and your friend will do the same for you.

STEP 3:
Then, FriendlyAlarm wakes you up by playing your friend's voice recorded in the previous step.

Be surprised, wake up, and begin your wonderful day!
